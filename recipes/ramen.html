Ramen

Bring the taste of East Asia to your kitchen!

Ingredients
-----------

  • 1 square of dry ramen noodles
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup fresh peas
  • 1/4 cup diced carrots
  • 1 boullion cube
  • 1/4 diced shitake mushrooms

Steps
-----

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a small pot on the stove
  2. Put the boullion cube in the water and allow it to dissolve
  3. Place the dry ramen, and carrots, and shitake in the boiling water
  4. When the ramen is al dente, turn the heat off but allow the pot to remain on the burner
  5. Crack the egg into the water, along with the peas, and allow the egg to cook for 2 minutes
  6. Drain the broth to preference and place contents into a bowl
  7. Enjoy!
