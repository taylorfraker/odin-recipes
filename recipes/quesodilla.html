Quesodilla

Enjoy this warm and crunchy taste of the southwest!

Ingredients
-----------

  • 2 white or whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded mozeralla cheese
  • 1/2 whole avocado, diced
  • 1/2 whole tomato, diced
  • 1 Tbsp butter

Steps
-----

  1. Place a medium sized flat frying pan on the stove. Medium heat.
  2. Melt and spread butter across the pan
  3. Place the tortilla in the pan
  4. Spread the cheese, tomato, and avocado on the tortilla
  5. Allow the cheese to begin to melt a bit before placing the second tortilla on top
  6. When the top tortilla begins to stick to the cheese, use a spatula to flip the quesodilla over
  7. Allow this side of the tortilla to brown for a couple minutes
  8. Place the quesodilla on a serving plated and use a pizza cutter to divide it into slices
  9. Enjoy!
