PBnJ

Bring a nostalgic tear to the eyes of your lunch guests with this classic childhood throwback! Just like mom used to make.

Ingredients
-----------

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 2 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp marionberry jam

Steps
-----

  1. Place the two slices of bread side by side on a flat surface
  2. Using a butter knife, spread the jam evenly across one of the slices
  3. Use the second piece of bread to wipe clean the knife
  4. Use the butter knife to spread the peanut butter across the second slice
  5. Place the slice with jam face down on top of the peanut butter covered slice
  6. Clean up and enjoy!
